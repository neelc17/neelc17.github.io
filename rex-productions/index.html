About Us

Who We Are

REX Productions is a high school film group that makes all kinds of videos ranging from short films to documentaries to music videos. We are all different: some are here for the love of the art, some for a release from the pressures of school. Everyone is here for some reason.

Our Goal

We want to show just how diverse the art of film-making is, and let you explore what aspects interest you. Creating a film requires knowledge of cameras, video editing, audio, graphics, writing, acting, and countless other skills, so everyone has a place. Everybody is different, but one thing that ties us all together at REX Productions is our passion for film-making, and we want to share it with you!

Special Events

At the end of the year, we hold REXFest, an event where we showcase some of our favorite films of the year, as well as winners of our short film contest.
